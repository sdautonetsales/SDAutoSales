About Us

Our goal is to save you time and money

We got what it takes

We are a family owned, licensed, and bonded auto broker based here in San Diego. With more than 15 years of experience in sales, finance, and customer service. We got what it takes to help you with your auto buying needs.

For most people, buying a vehicle is a major investment; probably next to buying a house. However, it entails a complicated process that most people do not want to deal with, because it’s not fun, it’s time consuming and in most cases pressure laden.

Our Company offers a simplified and cost-effective auto buying process that is appealing to our clientele. We do this by doing the leg work and by taking care of the negotiation, resulting to a streamlined auto buying process. We genuinely care about our client’s car buying experience because we want them to be our loyal customers.

WHAT WE OFFER

San Diego Auto Network would like to give your employees and their families the opportunity to save time and money on their next vehicle purchase by offering our new program.

FAQ Section

An Auto Buying Service Benefit Especially For You

Q. Why should we use your service?

A. Because we’ll save you time, money, and it’s free.

Q. Do we have to deal with sales people about the price?

A. No, you don’t have to haggle for the vehicle price. Since you will get the vehicle at a pre-arranged discounted price.

Q. What code should we use to avail of the discount?

A. Each participating company will be assigned a Group code no. Please use the code assigned to your company

Q. Are we buying from your company?

A. No, you will be buying directly from the dealer.

Q. What should I do once I’m ready to buy my next car?

A. Go to our website and fill out the order request form, or call us at (619) 817-5506

Q. Who will help me out during the buying process?

A. A manager at the participating dealership will help you complete the purchasing process.

Q. Should we contact you at the day of purchase?

A. Yes, because we want to make sure that you are completely satisfied with our service.

Q. Do you have employees at the dealership?

A. No, we don’t have employees at the dealerships but, your purchase will be facilitated by our contact manager

Q. What if problems arise during the purchase?

A. Problems should be handled by the dealer since you will purchase the vehicle directly from them. But we’ll do our best to help you out resolve should an issue arise.
